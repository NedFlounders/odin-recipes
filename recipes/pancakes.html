PANCAKES

Description

It's the simple pancake recipe that's gone viral worldwide - you'd 'batter' give it a go!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups self-raising flour
  • 1/3 cups caster sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, egg and vanilla together in a jug. Sift flour and bicarbonate of soda into a bowl. Stir in sugar. Make a well in centre. Add milk mixture. Whisk until just combined
  2. Heat a large non-stiuck frying pan over medium heat
  3. Add pancake mix
  4. Flip when bubbling
