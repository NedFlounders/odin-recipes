Ingredients

  • 3 and 1/4 cups Bread Flour
  • 2 teaspoons Instant Yeast
  • 2 teaspoons Coarse Salt
  • 1 and 1/2 cups Cool water

Steps

  1. Whisk flour, yeast and salt together. Pour in cool water and gently mix together
  2. Cover the dough tightly with plastic wrap and set on counter
  3. Dust baking sheet and lay out
  4. Bake in oven
